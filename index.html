Privacy Policy

DFT 101 – Design for Test is an educational app focused on teaching Design for Test (DFT) concepts used in VLSI and semiconductor design.

Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

  • No account creation
  • No analytics
  • No tracking
  • No ads
  • No third-party SDKs

Local Data (On-Device Only)

The app stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Topic completion status
  • Quiz scores and best scores
  • Progress information

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Third Parties

DFT 101 does not use advertising services, analytics services, social media integrations, or external tracking libraries.

Children’s Privacy

DFT 101 does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes

If this privacy policy changes, it will be updated on this page.
